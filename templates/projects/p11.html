Community Mobilization and Development

Community mobilization ensures citizens participation as stakeholders in decision-making processes – in partnership with local government where possible – to work together to develop their communities. SCHRPD works with local citizens to form community groups that expand public involvement. SCHRPD facilitates dialogue and cooperation by bringing together stakeholders for civic dialogue and mobilization on key priority issues.
