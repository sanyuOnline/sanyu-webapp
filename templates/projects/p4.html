LGBTI Rights

SCHRPD works to uphold the human rights of lesbians, gay, bisexual, transgender, intersex, and other people experiencing violence and discrimination based on sexual orientation or gender identity and expression. We collaborate with the in – country and diasporan human rights defenders focusing on countries whose laws, policies and attitude foster violence and discrimination.

SCHRPD works with them to address issues ranging from laws criminalizing same sex relationships and discrimination against LGBTI people for anti – LGBTI violence by documenting and reporting on them to the international community, advocating for change in legislation and pressing for greater protection.
